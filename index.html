OUR MOST POPULAR DISHES.

LIC restaurant has a variety of meals which are very popular in Cameroon, Italy, Nigeria. That is, LIC restaurant has branches in these three countries.

CAMEROONIAN MEALS

cameroonian cusine is a blend of traditional african dishes and french influences resolving to a unique and flavorful culinary experience. Some popular Cameroonian meals includes: Ndole, fufu, eru, mbongo choco, dibi, and even more

ITALIAN MEALS

Italian cusine is renowed for it's simplicity, freshness and regional diversity. Some popular Italian meals includes pizza, spaghetti, lasagna, risotto, osso buco, tiramisu and more

NIGERIAN MEALS

Nigerian cusine is a diverse blend of traditional dishes from differnt ethnic groups. Some popular Nigerian meals includes: Jellof rice, eba, suya, egusi soup, fufu pepper soup, banga soup, and more others
